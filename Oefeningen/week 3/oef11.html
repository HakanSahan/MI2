I'll help you find your blacksmith friend. You can stay the night in the barn. And tomorrow, I'll take you as far as the railroad tracks...you can follow them straight on in into town. I'll even give you a hat. (Maggie slams down plate at the kitchen counter, and crosses herself in the Catholic fashion.) That's great. Thanks.What about the locomotive? Grinning.
